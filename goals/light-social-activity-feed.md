# Feature Goal: Light Social and Activity Feed

## Problem

Public lists exist, but there is no lightweight social graph that helps users discover people and stay engaged through their activity.

## Goal

Add minimal social mechanics: follows and a simple activity feed based on public actions.

## User Value

- Better discovery of relevant curators and lists.
- More reasons to return through fresh activity.
- Social context without turning the product into a heavy social network.

## Core Scope (MVP)

- Follow / unfollow public users.
- Feed with recent public actions from followed users:
  - new public list,
  - major list update,
  - public rating activity.
- Entry points from profile and list pages.
- Basic feed filtering by action type.

## Product Rules

- Respect privacy and anonymous publishing settings.
- No private list events in feed.
- Feed should be chronological and easy to scan.
- Notifications are non-intrusive (in-app first).

## Out of Scope (Initial Version)

- Comments and direct messages.
- Complex recommendation ranking for feed ordering.

## Success Metrics

- Follow conversion rate from profile visits.
- Weekly active users consuming feed.
- Share of sessions with at least one social interaction.
